Economic data analysis

View code
Our goal in this project is to analyze economic data from the FRED API. We'll be looking to see if we can find any interesting trends related to unemployment in the U.S.
Technologies used: Python, Pandas, Numpy, Matplotlib, Plotly, Jupyter.
We created a bunch of different visualizations to better understand our data. Here, we see a bar graph showing unemployment rates at a given time, May 2020 for example, for various states.
Here, we see unemployment rates over time, from the 1980's to 2020's, for various states. We see that for the most part, all these lines generally follow each other.
And finally, we plotted each state's unemployment line graph against its participation rate line graph. It's interesting that not only do all of these states seem to have different behaviors with both their unemployment and participation rates, but also that there doesn't seem to be a discernible relationship between unemployment and participation that can be clearly extrapolated.
