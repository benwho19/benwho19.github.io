Hi, I'm Ben. I enjoy working with large, messy datasets to uncover actionable insights from them. I love the lightbulb-moment feeling of connecting the dots and the satisfaction of solving tough technical problems.

A little about me: I live in the Bay Area and was born and raised in San Francisco. In my spare time, I enjoy staying active, spending time with friends and family, and watching TV shows (some of my favorite shows are Arcane, House of the Dragon, and For All Mankind.)

I graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Cognitive Science, and during my time there, I was introduced to topics like computer science and data science. It was in these courses that I first tackled interesting projects such as determining the relationship between U.S. states' crime rates and capital punishment laws.

Afterwards, I found work as a software engineer - but after about a year, I realized that a data role might be better suited for me and I decided to make the switch. Many of the same things that attracted me to software engineering also attract me to data analysis, such as brainstorming creative solutions and utilizing code to solve real-world problems.

I'm excited for what the future holds and can't wait to dive in.
