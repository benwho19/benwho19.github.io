Hi, I'm Ben. I enjoy technical problem-solving, especially when it involves writing code and working with large datasets. The challenge of solving a difficult problem as well as the camaraderie of collaborating effectively with others are what ultimately bring me satisfaction when it comes to work.

A little about me: I live in the Bay Area and was born and raised in San Francisco. In my spare time, I enjoy staying active, spending time with friends and family, playing video games, and watching TV shows (some of my favorite shows are: Arcane, House of the Dragon, and For All Mankind.)

I graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Cognitive Science, and during my time there, I was introduced to topics like computer science and data science. It was in a data science course that I first found how eye-opening it was to see the kinds of previously-invisible relationships that data (combined with statistical techniques) can show you.

Afterwards, I found work as a software engineer - but after about a year, I realized that a data role might be better suited for me and I decided to make the switch. Many of the same things that attracted me to software engineering also attract me to data analysis, such as brainstorming creative solutions and utilizing code to solve technical problems. I'm excited for what the future holds and can't wait to dive in.
