Customer sales data analysis

View code
Our goal in this project is to conduct a data analysis on our customer sales dataset. To help us with our analysis, we'll use an RFM report, which is a method of segmenting customers using these three metrics:
  1. recency (how long ago their last purchase was),
  2. frequency (how often they purchase),
  3. and monetary value (how much they have spent total)
Technologies used: SQL, Tableau
So first, I did some analysis in SQL. I started off by inspecting the data and then preparing the data for analysis by grouping sales by product line.
I then ran queries to answer the following questions:
  • Which month was the best month for sales in a given year, and how much revenue was earned in that best month?
  • Which of the customers is the "best"? (Here is where I used RFM)
  • Which products are most often sold together?
  • What is the best product sold in the US?
  • Which city of a given country has the highest number of sales? (Picking the country arbitrarily)
After finishing this analysis, we find out things such as: November is the best month for sales (with $1,089,048), and within that month, classic cars had the most sales. The product codes that sold best together were: S50_1341, S700_1691, and S700_3167.
