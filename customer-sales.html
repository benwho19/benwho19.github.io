Customer sales data analysis

View code
Our goal in this project is to conduct a data analysis on our customer sales dataset. To help us with a part of our analysis, we'll use RFM, which is a method of segmenting customers using these three metrics:
  1. recency (how long ago their last purchase was),
  2. frequency (how often they purchase),
  3. and monetary value (how much they have spent total)
Technologies used: SQL, Tableau
First, we did some analysis in SQL. We started off by inspecting the data and then preparing the data for analysis by grouping sales by product line.
We then ran queries to answer the following questions:
  • Which month was the best month for sales in a given year, and how much revenue was earned in that best month?
  • Which of the customers is the "best"? (Here is where we used RFM)
  • Which products are most often sold together?
  • What is the best product sold in the US?
  • Which city of a given country has the highest number of sales? (Picking the country arbitrarily)
After finishing this analysis, some of our findings include:
  • November is the best month for sales (with $1,089,048)
  • Within the month of November, classic cars were the products with the most sales.
  • The product codes that sold best together were: S50_1341, S700_1691, and S700_3167.
